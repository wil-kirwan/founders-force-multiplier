## Core CRO Principles

### 1. Speed Is the Foundation
- Stores loading under 2s have 2.5x higher conversion rates than 5s loads
- A 0.1-second improvement can lift conversions 8.4%
- Every 1-second delay costs ~7% conversions
- Sites loading in 1 second convert 2.5-5x more than 5-10 second loads
- 70%+ of Shopify traffic is mobile — mobile speed is non-negotiable
- Top fixes: image optimization, app audit (remove unused apps), lazy loading, reduce fonts to 2 families max

### 2. Product Page Optimization
- Z-pattern layout: images left, key info right
- Above the fold: price, sticky Add-to-Cart button (contrasting color), trust badges
- Social proof directly above ATC button (review cards with star ratings)
- Benefit-driven copy first, then specs: "Wear it daily without worry — water & sweat resistant"
- Accordions for supporting details (sizing, shipping, materials, care)
- High-quality multi-angle images + lifestyle shots (5-8 minimum, top brands 8-12)
- Product videos increase ATC by 144%
- High-quality photos convert up to 94% better than poor ones

### 3. Checkout Optimization (70% Abandon Here)
- Guest checkout is mandatory — 63% abandon if forced to create account
- Show shipping costs early (surprise fees = #1 abandonment reason)
- 18% of users abandon due to checkout UX being too long/complicated
- Offer multiple payment methods (Shop Pay, Apple Pay, BNPL, PayPal)
- Progress indicators + minimal form fields
- Mobile checkout must be frictionless (5x more likely to abandon on unoptimized mobile)

### 4. Trust Architecture
- Reviews lift conversions 3-37% depending on volume
- Trust badges below ATC button: secure checkout, guarantees, certifications
- Money-back guarantee badges near ATC and checkout buttons
- Transparent return policies visible on product pages (not buried in footer)
- "As Seen In" banners with press/influencer logos
- Real customer photos in reviews build strongest trust
- Star ratings near product titles for quick social proof scanning

### 5. Urgency & Scarcity (Used Carefully)
- "Only X left in stock" notifications on low-inventory items
- Countdown timers for seasonal promotions (don't overuse — savvy buyers see through fake scarcity)
- Bundle deals to increase AOV (proven 20-34% AOV lift)
- Free shipping thresholds drive larger cart sizes
- "Best Seller" and "New" badges on collection grid cards

### 6. Backend & Retention Systems
- Abandoned cart email sequences: 3 emails at 1hr, 24hr, 72hr
  - Email 1: Reminder with product image
  - Email 2: Social proof + reviews
  - Email 3: Small incentive (5-10% off or free shipping)
- Browse abandonment emails (viewed but didn't add to cart)
- Exit-intent popups (10% off first order for email capture)
- Post-purchase flows to increase CLV
- Order confirmation email optimization (one brand: $0 to $4,200/mo revenue)
- Backend systems can convert 30-40% more orders

### 7. Landing Page Strategy
- Pages with ONE clear CTA generate 266% more leads than multi-offer pages
- Top performers remove navigation bars (84% have them, best pages don't)
- Structure: Problem → Proof → Solution
- Dedicated landing pages hit 10-15% CR vs ~1.4% average store rate
- Match the buying intent (gifting, self-care, seasonal, etc.)
- Smart bundles on landing pages boost AOV 20-34%

### 8. BNPL (Buy Now, Pay Later)
- Increases AOV 20-30%
- Display "or 4 interest-free payments of $XX" below price on every product page
- Shop Pay Installments is a quick toggle for Shopify stores
- Afterpay and Klarna popular in Canada/US/UK/AU

### 9. A/B Testing Framework
- Use ICE (Impact, Confidence, Ease) or PIE (Potential, Importance, Ease) to prioritize tests
- Test one variable at a time: CTAs, hero images, product titles, pricing display
- CRO yields 3-8x ROI compared to paid acquisition

## CRO Audit Checklist (Quick Reference)

When auditing any Shopify/ecommerce store, check for:

**Product Pages:**
- [ ] Reviews/ratings system installed and displaying
- [ ] Product descriptions on ALL products (materials, dimensions, care, story)
- [ ] High-quality images (5-8 minimum, multiple angles + lifestyle)
- [ ] Prominent ATC button (contrasting color, sticky on mobile)
- [ ] Trust badges below ATC
- [ ] BNPL messaging below price
- [ ] Expandable accordions for details
- [ ] Cross-sell / "Style With" section
- [ ] Breadcrumb navigation

**Homepage:**
- [ ] Clear value proposition above the fold
- [ ] Product grid visible (Best Sellers with prices + Quick Add)
- [ ] Value prop bar (shipping, guarantees, USPs)
- [ ] Social proof section
- [ ] Current/relevant hero (not outdated seasonal)

**Collection Pages:**
- [ ] Star ratings on product cards
- [ ] Quick Add functionality
- [ ] Filtering (price, size, color, material)
- [ ] Sorting (best sellers default)
- [ ] Hover image swap
- [ ] Collection descriptions for SEO

**Technical:**
- [ ] Page load under 3 seconds (ideally under 2)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized (responsive, thumb-friendly, sticky ATC)
- [ ] Minimal script bloat (audit installed apps)
- [ ] Only 1 email platform (not multiple)
- [ ] Max 2 font families

**Checkout & Cart:**
- [ ] Guest checkout enabled
- [ ] Multiple payment methods visible
- [ ] Shipping costs shown early
- [ ] Free shipping progress bar in cart
- [ ] Cart upsells/cross-sells

**Email & Recovery:**
- [ ] Abandoned cart email sequence (3 emails)
- [ ] Browse abandonment emails
- [ ] Exit-intent popup with incentive
- [ ] Post-purchase review request flow
- [ ] Welcome series for new subscribers

**Policies & Trust:**
- [ ] Return/exchange policy prominent (not just in footer)
- [ ] FAQ page exists and covers common questions
- [ ] Shipping timeline visible on product pages
- [ ] Payment method icons displayed
