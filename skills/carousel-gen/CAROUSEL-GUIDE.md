# Carousel Content Strategy Guide

Read this file before writing ANY carousel content. Every slide must follow these rules.

---

## The Triple Hook Framework

Instagram gives carousels 3 separate chances to hook each viewer. When someone doesn't engage with slide 1, the algorithm shows them slide 2 next time, then slide 3. This means:

- **Slides 1, 2, and 3 must each work as a STANDALONE hook** for a cold scroller with zero context
- They are three separate entry points into the same content
- They should progress the topic without progressing too much - each reveals a different angle
- Max 10 words per hook headline
- Each hook should trigger a different emotion: curiosity, fear of missing out, desire to learn

---

## Hook Formulas (Ranked by Save Rate)

1. **"X things I wish I knew about [topic]"** - Curiosity + regret avoidance
2. **"Stop doing [mistake]... do this instead"** - Pattern interrupt + solution
3. **"The [adjective] guide to [topic]"** - Authority + comprehensiveness
4. **"[Number]-step framework for [outcome]"** - Structure + promise
5. **"I [achievement] in [timeframe]. Here's how:"** - Proof + method
6. **"[Tool/person] just changed everything"** - News + impact
7. **"Most people get [topic] wrong. Here's why."** - Contrarian + insight
8. **"The difference between [A] and [B]"** - Comparison + clarity

---

## CTA Formulas (Ranked by Engagement)

1. **"Save this for when you need it"** - Drives saves (algorithm priority #1)
2. **"Send this to someone who [needs X]"** - Drives shares to DMs (#2 signal)
3. **"DM me '[keyword]' for [free resource]"** - Lead generation
4. **"Follow @[handle] for daily [topic] tips"** - Follower growth
5. **"Drop a [emoji] if you're trying this"** - Comment engagement
6. **"Which one surprised you most? Comment below"** - Discussion driver

---

## Content Arc Templates

### Educational (most common)
Hook > Problem > Solution > Proof > CTA
- Best for: tutorials, how-tos, tool breakdowns
- Slide count: 7-10

### Listicle
Hook > Item 1 > Item 2 > ... > Summary > CTA
- Best for: tool roundups, tip collections, resource lists
- Slide count: 8-12

### Story
Hook > Setup > Conflict > Resolution > CTA
- Best for: case studies, personal experiences, transformations
- Slide count: 6-8

### Myth-Busting
Hook > Common Belief > Why It's Wrong > Better Approach > CTA
- Best for: contrarian takes, misconception correction
- Slide count: 6-8

---

## Slide Copy Rules

### Hook slides (1-3)
- Max 10 words per headline
- Each works standalone for cold scrollers
- No context dependencies between hooks
- Must trigger: curiosity, urgency, or desire

### Content slides (4+)
- One idea per slide - never two
- Max 8 word headline
- Max 25 word body text
- Grade 6 reading level (simple, direct)
- Lead with the insight, not the setup
- Use specific numbers over vague claims ("47%" not "many")

### CTA slide (final)
- Clear single action
- Include handle prominently
- Reinforce the save/share value

---

## Algorithm Signal Priorities

What Instagram rewards, in order:
1. **Saves** - strongest signal, means "I'll come back to this"
2. **Shares to DMs** - second strongest, means "someone else needs this"
3. **Swipe-through rate** - how many slides people view
4. **Dwell time** - how long they spend on each slide
5. **Comments** - engagement depth
6. **Likes** - weakest signal

Design every carousel to maximize saves and shares first. Adding music makes carousels eligible for the Reels tab.

---

## Viral Optimization Checklist

Before finalizing any carousel, verify:

- [ ] Triple Hook check: slides 1, 2, 3 each work standalone
- [ ] Hook strength: each hook is under 10 words and triggers emotion
- [ ] One idea per slide: no slide tries to do two things
- [ ] Specificity: uses concrete numbers, tools, names - not vague claims
- [ ] Save-worthy: would someone bookmark this to reference later?
- [ ] Share-worthy: would someone send this to a friend or colleague?
- [ ] Surprising insight: at least one slide reveals something non-obvious
- [ ] Clear CTA: final slide has one unmistakable action
- [ ] Reading level: body text is grade 6 or below
- [ ] Slide count: 7-10 slides for educational, 8-12 for listicles

## Caption Formula

```
[1-2 sentence hook that restates the carousel value]

[3-5 bullet points summarizing key takeaways]

Save this for later. Share with someone who needs it.

Follow @[handle] for more [topic] content.

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3 #hashtag4 #hashtag5
```

Hashtag rules:
- 5-10 hashtags max (not 30)
- Mix: 2 broad (#AI #productivity), 2 niche (#claudeai #aitools), 1-2 trending
- Place at end of caption, not inline
